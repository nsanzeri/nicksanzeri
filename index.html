Thanks for stopping by

Nick Sanzeri is a multi-instrumentalist with a flair for writing, arranging and producing. His earliest years were spent writing and singing and playing bass with various line-ups. He went on to study music composition at Columbia College Chicago, and continues to play on countless studio sessions for various artists and other commercial work, as well as being a permanent staple of the exciting live scene of Chicago and the surrounding areas. Follow the Feeling filters his love of all things poppy, funky and quirky through the lens of humor which have become his trademarks.
